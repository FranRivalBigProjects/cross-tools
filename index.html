❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is io_uring faster than epoll?

io_uring can provide lower latency and higher throughput, especially in high-performance applications.

Which compiler is faster: GCC or Clang?

Clang is often faster for compilation, while GCC may produce more optimized binaries in some cases.

Is gRPC better than REST?

gRPC is generally faster and more efficient, while REST is simpler and more widely supported.

Does SIMD always improve performance?

SIMD can significantly improve performance for parallel workloads, but not all tasks benefit from it.
